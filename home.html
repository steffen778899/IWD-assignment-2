Introduction

Welcome to our online book store!

Auckland City Books was established in 1971 and we have our book stores in every little town in New Zealand. We stock books on a wide variety of subjects including Art, Drama, Humour, Music, Photography, Biography, Sports, Business, Careers, Investing, Cooking and Food, Education, Fiction and Poetry, Travel, Health and Self-Help, Medicine, Nutrition, Psychology, Recovery and Addiction, Relationships, Gardening, Interior Design, Sustainable Living, Humanities.

The books sold on this website are the same as sold in our stores, but you don't need to go to the stores any more. Take your time and explore what you want to read while drinking a cup of coffee!
